## What Causes a Hangover (And Why IV Therapy Works)

Understanding the science behind hangovers reveals why **IV therapy for hangover recovery** outperforms every over-the-counter remedy. Alcohol triggers multiple physiological disruptions that IV therapy addresses simultaneously:

**Dehydration:** Alcohol is a powerful diuretic, causing your kidneys to eliminate fluids faster than you can replace them through drinking. In Santa Monica's sunny climate, where heat and salt air already pull moisture from your body, alcohol-induced dehydration hits even harder — resulting in pounding headaches, dizziness, and that cotton-mouth sensation.

**Electrolyte Imbalance:** As you lose fluids, critical electrolytes like sodium, potassium, and magnesium flush out with them. This imbalance contributes to muscle cramps, fatigue, and irregular heartbeat.

**Nutrient Depletion:** Processing alcohol burns through B vitamins, Vitamin C, and other essential nutrients. This depletion manifests as brain fog, mood disturbances, and persistent fatigue.

**Toxin Accumulation:** Your liver converts alcohol into acetaldehyde — a toxic compound significantly more harmful than alcohol itself. This buildup causes nausea, inflammation, and that overall toxic feeling.

**Inflammatory Response:** Alcohol triggers body-wide inflammation, contributing to headaches, body aches, and general malaise.

**Why IV Therapy Dominates Oral Solutions:**
When you're hungover, your digestive system often rebels against food or drink. Nausea makes keeping down water nearly impossible, and even when you can, your compromised gut absorbs nutrients slowly and inefficiently. **Hangover IV therapy** bypasses your digestive system completely, delivering fluids, electrolytes, vitamins, and medications directly into your bloodstream for immediate cellular access and relief.
