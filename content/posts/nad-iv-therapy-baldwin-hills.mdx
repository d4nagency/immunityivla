### Cellular Energy Production

NAD+ is essential for mitochondrial function—the cellular powerhouses responsible for producing ATP, your body's primary energy currency. Our Baldwin Hills NAD+ IV therapy directly addresses:

**Mitochondrial Biogenesis** — Stimulation of new mitochondrial creation, essentially upgrading your cellular energy infrastructure for long-term vitality

### DNA Repair and Longevity

The DNA in every cell undergoes thousands of damage events daily from environmental factors, metabolic processes, and normal wear and tear. NAD+ is essential for the enzymes that repair this damage:

**Sirtuin Activation** — NAD+ activates sirtuins, the "longevity genes" that repair DNA, regulate metabolism, and extend cellular lifespan. Research indicates sirtuin activation may contribute significantly to longevity.

**PARP Function** — Poly (ADP-ribose) polymerases (PARPs) are DNA repair enzymes that require NAD+ to function. Adequate NAD+ levels ensure your cells can effectively repair genetic damage.

**Telomere Protection** — Emerging research suggests NAD+ may help maintain telomere length—the protective caps on DNA chromosomes that shorten with age, contributing to cellular aging.

### Neuroprotection and Cognitive Function

Baldwin Hills professionals and entrepreneurs rely on sharp cognitive function for career success. NAD+ provides crucial support for brain health:

**Neuronal Energy Support** — The brain consumes 20% of your body's energy despite comprising only 2% of body weight. NAD+ ensures neurons have the fuel to function optimally.

**Neuroinflammation Reduction** — Chronic low-grade neuroinflammation accelerates cognitive decline. NAD+ helps regulate inflammatory responses in brain tissue.

**Neurotransmitter Synthesis** — NAD+ is required for the synthesis of dopamine, serotonin, and other neurotransmitters essential for mood, motivation, and cognitive clarity.
