## The Science of NAD+: What is it?

Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide (NAD+) is a coenzyme found in every single living cell in your body. It plays a fundamental role in many essential processes, most notably in energy metabolism and DNA repair. Think of NAD+ as the fuel that keeps your cellular engines running.

As we age, our natural levels of NAD+ begin to decline. This decline is often linked to the various hallmarks of aging, including reduced energy, cognitive fog, decreased metabolic efficiency, and the breakdown of cellular repair mechanisms. By utilizing IV therapy to replenish these levels, we bypass the digestive system, ensuring that the NAD+ reaches your bloodstream with maximum bioavailability and immediate impact.
