**How much does Myers' Cocktail IV therapy cost in Monterey Park?**

**Is Myers' Cocktail safe for everyone?**
The Myers' Cocktail is generally safe for healthy adults. Our licensed clinicians conduct health screenings before every treatment. Certain conditions like kidney disease require modified protocols — we'll discuss your individual situation when you book.

**How long does the Myers' Cocktail IV take?**
Plan for 60-75 minutes total — approximately 5 minutes for setup, 45-60 minutes for the infusion, and a few minutes for aftercare. The process is comfortable and many clients use the time productively.

**How often should I get a Myers' Cocktail?**
Many clients benefit from monthly treatments for maintenance. During high-stress periods, you might schedule weekly sessions. For specific concerns like recovery or preparation periods, we can recommend customized schedules.

**How quickly will I feel results?**
Most clients report feeling more energetic within 24-48 hours. Some experience immediate effects during or immediately after treatment. Benefits typically last 1-2 weeks depending on your nutritional status and lifestyle factors.

**What's the difference between Classic and Enhanced Myers' Cocktail?**
The Enhanced Plus formula contains higher concentrations of key nutrients plus additional glutathione and selenium. It's ideal for those with higher nutritional needs, during recovery periods, or when preparing for demanding events.

**Can I get treatment in my apartment or condo?**
Absolutely. Our mobile nurses work in all types of residences throughout Monterey Park — single-family homes, apartments, condos, and senior living facilities. We bring everything needed for safe, comfortable treatment.

**Do you treat multiple family members in one visit?**

**Is IV therapy better than taking vitamins orally?**
IV delivery bypasses the digestive system, allowing 100% nutrient absorption directly into your bloodstream. Oral supplements typically provide only 20-50% absorption and can cause digestive upset at therapeutic doses.
