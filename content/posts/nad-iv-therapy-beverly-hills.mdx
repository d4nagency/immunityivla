## The Science Behind NAD+ Therapy

### Understanding Cellular Energy and Aging

NAD+ functions as a crucial coenzyme in three fundamental biological processes:

**1. Cellular Energy Production (ATP Synthesis)**

Every cell in your body requires adenosine triphosphate (ATP) to function. NAD+ is essential for the electron transport chain—the cellular machinery that generates ATP. When NAD+ levels decline, energy production suffers, resulting in fatigue, brain fog, and reduced metabolic function. NAD+ IV therapy restores this fundamental energy pathway.

**2. DNA Repair and Cellular Maintenance**

Our DNA suffers damage daily from environmental stressors, normal metabolic processes, and aging. NAD+ activates sirtuins and PARPs—enzymes that repair DNA damage and maintain cellular integrity. Without adequate NAD+, DNA damage accumulates, contributing to aging and disease risk. IV NAD+ provides the resources your cells need for ongoing maintenance and repair.

**3. Cellular Communication and Signaling**

NAD+ serves as a signaling molecule, helping cells communicate about their energy status and respond appropriately to stress. This signaling function affects metabolism, circadian rhythms, and cellular stress responses. Restoring NAD+ optimizes these communication pathways for improved overall function.

### Why IV Administration Matters

Oral NAD+ supplements face significant challenges:

**Digestive Breakdown** — Stomach acids and enzymes destroy much of the NAD+ molecule before absorption
**Poor Bioavailability** — Even specialized formulations achieve only 10-20% absorption
**First-Pass Metabolism** — The liver processes oral supplements, further reducing available NAD+
**Delayed Effects** — Oral supplementation takes weeks or months to noticeably affect cellular levels
