## The Science of Recovery: Why You Feel This Way

To understand why a **hangover IV drip in Marina del Rey** is so effective, it's important to understand what happens to your body after a night of drinking. Alcohol is a potent diuretic, meaning it encourages your kidneys to release more water than they would otherwise. This leads to systemic dehydration, which is a primary cause of the classic hangover symptoms: fatigue, dizziness, and those dreaded pounding headaches.

Furthermore, alcohol consumption disrupts the balance of electrolytes in your body—minerals like sodium, potassium, and magnesium that are critical for nerve function, muscle contraction, and fluid balance. When these levels drop, you feel sluggish, nauseous, and physically weak. While drinking water or sports drinks might seem like a solution, the digestive system can only process fluids so quickly. Intravenous (IV) therapy bypasses the gut entirely, delivering fluids and nutrients directly into your bloodstream for near-instantaneous absorption.
