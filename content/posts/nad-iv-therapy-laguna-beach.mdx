## Understanding NAD+: The Cellular Powerhouse

NAD+ is often called the "fountain of youth" molecule, and for good reason. This essential coenzyme plays a critical role in virtually every cellular process in your body:

### Cellular Energy Production
NAD+ is a key component of cellular respiration—the process by which your cells convert nutrients into ATP (adenosine triphosphate), the energy currency of life. Without adequate NAD+, your cells simply cannot produce the energy needed for optimal function. This is why NAD+ deficiency manifests as fatigue, brain fog, and reduced physical performance.

### DNA Repair and Protection
Every day, your DNA sustains thousands of damage events from environmental factors, metabolic processes, and natural aging. NAD+ activates enzymes called sirtuins and PARPs that detect and repair this damage. Higher NAD+ levels support more efficient DNA repair, helping protect against mutations and cellular dysfunction that accelerate aging.

### Sirtuin Activation
Sirtuins are a family of proteins sometimes called "longevity genes" because of their role in regulating aging and age-related diseases. These proteins require NAD+ to function. By boosting NAD+ levels, you essentially activate your body's natural anti-aging mechanisms at the cellular level.

### Mitochondrial Health
Mitochondria are the powerhouses within your cells, and their health directly impacts your energy, metabolism, and vitality. NAD+ supports mitochondrial function and biogenesis (the creation of new mitochondria), ensuring your cells have the energy factories they need to thrive.

### Metabolic Function
NAD+ plays a critical role in regulating metabolic processes, including glucose metabolism and insulin sensitivity. For Laguna Beach residents who prioritize fitness and body composition, optimal NAD+ levels support healthy metabolism and energy balance.
