## Understanding Immune Boost IV Therapy

### What's in an Immune Boost IV?

**Vitamin C (High Dose)**
The cornerstone of immune IV therapy, Vitamin C is essential for immune cell function. At therapeutic doses (typically 10-25 grams delivered intravenously), Vitamin C stimulates the production and function of white blood cells — your body's primary defenders against infection. Oral Vitamin C cannot achieve these concentrations due to digestive limitations.

**Zinc Sulfate**
This essential mineral is critical for immune cell development and communication. Zinc deficiency profoundly affects immune function, and even mild deficiency can impair your body's ability to fight infections. IV delivery ensures optimal zinc levels are achieved quickly.

**Selenium**
An often-overlooked trace mineral, selenium is essential for antioxidant defense and proper immune responses. It helps regulate inflammation and supports the function of various immune cells.

**B-Complex Vitamins**
Your immune system is metabolically demanding — it requires significant energy to mount effective responses. B-vitamins fuel these processes, support healthy mucous membranes (a first line of defense), and help manage the stress that suppresses immune function.

**Glutathione (Optional Add-On)**
Often called the "master antioxidant," glutathione plays a crucial role in immune function and detoxification. Adding glutathione to your Immune Boost IV provides powerful cellular protection and supports your body's natural defense mechanisms.
