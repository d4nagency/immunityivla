**How often should I get an Immune Boost IV?**

Yes, and this is actually an ideal time for Immune Boost IV therapy. Early intervention at the first sign of illness can help support your immune response and potentially reduce severity. If you have a fever over 101°F or severe symptoms, we recommend consulting your primary physician first.

**How does Immune Boost IV compare to oral supplements?**
IV delivery bypasses the digestive system, providing 100% bioavailability versus 20-50% absorption for oral supplements. IV therapy also achieves therapeutic blood concentrations impossible with oral dosing, delivering immediate immune support.

**Can I ride horses after my IV treatment?**
Yes, normal physical activity can typically resume immediately after treatment. However, we recommend avoiding strenuous riding or competition for the remainder of the day to allow your body to optimally process the nutrients.
