## The Science of NAD+ IV Therapy

### Understanding NAD+ and Cellular Health

NAD+ is arguably the most critical coenzyme for cellular health and longevity. Present in every living cell, NAD+ serves three essential functions:

**1. Energy Production** — NAD+ is essential for mitochondrial function, the cellular powerhouses that produce ATP (adenosine triphosphate), your body's energy currency. Without adequate NAD+, cellular energy production declines, leading to fatigue and reduced function. IV NAD+ rapidly restores mitochondrial efficiency, often resulting in noticeable energy improvements within hours to days.

**2. DNA Repair & Cellular Maintenance** — NAD+ activates enzymes called PARPs (Poly ADP-Ribose Polymerases) that detect and repair DNA damage. It also activates sirtuins, the so-called "longevity genes" that regulate cellular health, inflammation, and aging processes. NAD+ IV therapy supports your body's natural DNA repair mechanisms, potentially slowing cellular aging.

**3. Cell Signaling** — NAD+ functions as a signaling molecule that helps cells communicate and coordinate responses to stress, nutrient availability, and environmental changes. This signaling role is crucial for metabolic regulation and cellular adaptation.

### Why IV Delivery is Superior

While oral NAD+ precursors (like NMN and NR) are available, IV administration offers significant advantages:

**Direct Bioavailability** — IV delivery bypasses the digestive system, delivering NAD+ directly to your bloodstream with 100% bioavailability. Oral supplements must survive stomach acid and pass through the liver, significantly reducing the amount that reaches your cells.

**Rapid Cellular Uptake** — Bloodstream delivery allows NAD+ to reach cells throughout your body quickly. Many clients report feeling effects during or shortly after their first infusion.

**Higher Dose Delivery** — IV therapy can deliver therapeutic doses (250-750mg) that would be impractical or impossible to achieve through oral supplementation alone.

**Sustained Elevation** — IV NAD+ creates sustained elevation of NAD+ levels in tissues for several days to weeks, providing longer-lasting benefits than oral alternatives.
