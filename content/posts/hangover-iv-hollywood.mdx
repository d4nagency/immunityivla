## What Causes a Hangover (And Why IV Therapy Works)

Understanding the science behind hangovers explains why **IV therapy for hangover recovery** is so effective. Alcohol consumption triggers a cascade of physiological effects:

**Dehydration:** Alcohol is a diuretic, causing your body to lose fluids and electrolytes faster than you can replace them by drinking water. This leads to the pounding headaches, dry mouth, and dizziness that define the hangover experience.

**Nutrient Depletion:** Your body burns through B vitamins and other essential nutrients while processing alcohol. This depletion contributes to fatigue, brain fog, and that general sense of "feeling off."

**Toxin Buildup:** As your liver breaks down alcohol, it produces acetaldehyde — a toxic compound responsible for many hangover symptoms including nausea and inflammation.

**Inflammation:** Alcohol triggers an inflammatory response throughout your body, contributing to that overall achy, unwell feeling.

**Why IV Therapy Beats Oral Remedies:**
When you're hungover, your digestive system is often compromised. Nausea makes it hard to keep down water or supplements, and even when you can, absorption through your gut is slow and inefficient. **Hangover IV therapy** bypasses your digestive system entirely, delivering fluids, electrolytes, vitamins, and medications directly into your bloodstream for immediate effect.
