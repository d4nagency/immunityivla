## How Hangover IV Therapy Works

### The Science of Rapid Recovery

Alcohol creates hangovers through multiple mechanisms that oral remedies simply can't address effectively:

**Dehydration** — Alcohol inhibits vasopressin, causing your body to lose fluids at an accelerated rate. IV fluids restore hydration immediately, whereas drinking water relies on a compromised digestive system.

**Electrolyte Imbalance** — Alcohol depletes essential minerals including magnesium, potassium, and sodium. Our IV formula includes balanced electrolytes for rapid restoration.

**Toxin Buildup** — Your liver produces acetaldehyde while processing alcohol, causing nausea, headaches, and fatigue. IV glutathione and supportive nutrients accelerate toxin clearance.

**Nutrient Depletion** — Alcohol depletes B vitamins crucial for energy and neurological function. Direct IV delivery restores these nutrients instantly rather than relying on absorption through an irritated stomach.

**Inflammation** — Alcohol triggers inflammatory responses throughout the body. IV antioxidants and anti-inflammatory medications provide immediate relief.
