## The Science of NAD+ and Longevity

NAD+ is essential for several critical cellular processes:

### Energy Production
NAD+ is a key component in mitochondrial function — the process by which your cells generate ATP (adenosine triphosphate), the body's primary energy currency. Higher NAD+ levels translate to improved cellular energy, reduced fatigue, and enhanced physical performance.

### DNA Repair
Every day, your DNA sustains thousands of damage events from environmental factors, metabolic processes, and natural aging. NAD+ activates enzymes called sirtuins and PARPs that detect and repair this damage, helping maintain genomic stability and potentially slowing the aging process.

### Cellular Signaling
NAD+ acts as a signaling molecule that regulates metabolism, circadian rhythms, and stress responses. Optimal NAD+ levels support better sleep patterns, improved metabolic health, and enhanced stress adaptation.

### Neuroprotection
The brain is particularly vulnerable to NAD+ decline. Research suggests that NAD+ supplementation may support cognitive function, protect against neurodegenerative processes, and promote mental clarity — benefits that resonate with Bel Air's accomplished professionals and creatives.
