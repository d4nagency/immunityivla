## The Science of Hangover IV Therapy

### Why IV Therapy Works Faster Than Traditional Remedies

When you're hungover, your stomach is often too irritated to process oral medications and supplements effectively. You might drink water only to have it come back up. You take pain relievers on an empty stomach, risking further irritation. IV therapy bypasses your gastrointestinal system entirely, delivering relief directly into your bloodstream where it can work immediately.

Studies show that IV hydration can restore fluid balance and blood volume within 30-45 minutes, while drinking the equivalent amount of water would take hours and risk further stomach upset. The medications we include—Zofran for nausea, Toradol for pain, Pepcid for stomach acid—work within minutes when administered intravenously versus 30-60+ minutes for oral versions.

### Addressing All Four Hangover Pathways

Alcohol creates hangovers through multiple mechanisms, and our IV protocol targets each one:

**1. Dehydration** — Alcohol inhibits vasopressin, the hormone that prevents fluid loss. Our lactated Ringer's solution directly restores blood volume and cellular hydration.

**2. Electrolyte Imbalance** — Alcohol acts as a diuretic, flushing essential minerals from your system. Our IV contains precisely balanced electrolytes to restore proper cellular function.

**3. Gastrointestinal Irritation** — Alcohol increases stomach acid and delays stomach emptying. Our anti-nausea and anti-heartburn medications provide immediate GI relief.

**4. Inflammation & Toxic Metabolites** — Your body breaks alcohol into acetaldehyde, a toxic compound that causes headaches, flushing, and malaise. Our antioxidant blend—including glutathione and vitamin C—supports your liver in processing these toxins while anti-inflammatories reduce systemic inflammation.
