## The Science Behind NAD+ and Aging

**NAD+ is fundamental to human health at the cellular level.** This essential coenzyme plays critical roles in:

- **Mitochondrial function:** Converting nutrients into cellular energy (ATP)
- **DNA repair:** Activating sirtuins and PARP enzymes that maintain genomic integrity
- **Cellular communication:** Supporting signaling pathways that regulate metabolism and circadian rhythms
- **Gene expression:** Influencing which genes are turned on or off throughout your lifetime

As we age, declining NAD+ levels impair these vital functions, leading to the hallmarks of aging: decreased energy, slower metabolism, cognitive fog, reduced physical performance, and compromised cellular repair mechanisms. Research from institutions including Harvard Medical School and MIT has demonstrated that replenishing NAD+ levels can reverse aspects of cellular aging and restore more youthful function.
