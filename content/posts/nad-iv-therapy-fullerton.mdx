## The Science Behind NAD+ IV Therapy

Understanding how NAD+ works helps Fullerton residents appreciate why this isn't just another wellness trend — it's foundational cellular biology.

### What Is NAD+?

**Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide** exists in two forms: NAD+ (oxidized) and NADH (reduced). This molecule shuttles electrons during cellular respiration — the process converting food into usable energy. Without adequate NAD+, your cells literally cannot produce sufficient ATP, regardless of how well you eat or sleep.

NAD+ also functions as a signaling molecule, triggering cellular repair processes when DNA damage occurs and activating sirtuins that regulate metabolism and aging.

### Why Do NAD+ Levels Decline?

Research shows NAD+ levels drop approximately 50% between age 20 and age 50. Contributing factors include:

- **Increased consumption by CD38:** This immune enzyme consumes NAD+ more aggressively as we age
- **Decreased biosynthesis:** Aging cells become less efficient at producing NAD+ from dietary precursors
- **DNA damage accumulation:** Repair processes consume NAD+, and damage accumulates faster than it can be repaired
- **Chronic inflammation:** Low-grade chronic inflammation — common in modern lifestyles — depletes NAD+ reserves
- **Poor diet:** Excessive alcohol, processed foods, and nutrient deficiencies impair NAD+ synthesis

### Why IV Delivery Matters

Oral NAD+ supplements exist but face significant bioavailability challenges. The digestive system breaks down most NAD+ into precursor molecules (nicotinamide riboside or nicotinamide mononucleotide), and digestive enzymes destroy much of what's swallowed. Studies suggest oral NAD+ raises blood levels minimally — perhaps 5-10% compared to baseline.

**IV administration** bypasses the gastrointestinal tract entirely. NAD+ enters your bloodstream directly, achieving plasma concentrations 100-200x higher than oral supplementation. This flood of NAD+ saturates your cells, providing immediate substrate for energy production, DNA repair, and sirtuin activation.

Fullerton residents report that IV NAD+ produces noticeable benefits — sustained energy, mental clarity, improved mood — that oral supplements never achieved despite months of use.
