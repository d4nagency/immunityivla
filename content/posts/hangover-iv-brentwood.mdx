## The Science of Rapid Hangover Recovery

A hangover is primarily caused by several physiological factors:

1. **Dehydration:** Alcohol is a diuretic, causing your body to lose fluids and essential electrolytes.
2. **Electrolyte Imbalance:** The loss of sodium, potassium, and magnesium can lead to muscle aches, fatigue, and brain fog.
3. **Inflammation and Acetaldehyde:** The breakdown of alcohol produces acetaldehyde, a toxic byproduct that contributes to nausea and headaches.
4. **Nutrient Depletion:** Alcohol consumption can deplete vital B vitamins and antioxidants.
