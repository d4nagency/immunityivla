## The Science Behind NAD+ and Longevity

NAD+ is essential for multiple critical biological processes:

### Mitochondrial Energy Production
NAD+ is fundamental to mitochondrial function — the powerhouses that generate ATP, your cells' energy currency. Higher NAD+ levels translate to improved energy, reduced fatigue, and enhanced physical endurance — benefits that San Diego's active residents particularly appreciate.

### DNA Repair and Cellular Maintenance
Daily environmental exposures, metabolic processes, and natural aging damage your DNA thousands of times per day. NAD+ activates sirtuins and PARP enzymes that detect and repair this damage, supporting genomic stability and potentially slowing the biological aging process.

### Cognitive Function and Neuroprotection
The brain requires substantial NAD+ for optimal function. Research indicates that NAD+ supplementation may support memory, mental clarity, and cognitive performance — advantages valued by San Diego's professionals, entrepreneurs, and students.

### Metabolic Health
NAD+ regulates metabolism, circadian rhythms, and stress responses. Optimal levels support healthy metabolism, better sleep quality, and improved stress adaptation — foundations of the balanced wellness lifestyle San Diego residents embrace.
