## How Hangover IV Therapy Works

That miserable morning-after feeling isn't just about drinking too much—it's about what alcohol does to your body. Understanding this process explains why IV therapy works so much better than home remedies.

**Dehydration: The Root of Hangover Misery**
Alcohol is a diuretic, meaning it forces your body to expel fluids faster than you can replace them. Every bathroom trip during a night out strips away essential hydration. By morning, your body is significantly dehydrated, causing headaches, dizziness, dry mouth, and fatigue. Oral rehydration takes hours because your compromised digestive system absorbs liquids slowly—including the very water you're trying to drink to feel better.

**Nutrient Depletion and Inflammation**
Alcohol processing consumes enormous amounts of B vitamins, Vitamin C, magnesium, and other nutrients essential for energy production and cellular function. Simultaneously, alcohol triggers inflammatory responses throughout your body, contributing to that achy, sick feeling that makes even simple tasks feel monumental.
**Toxin Buildup**
Your liver works overtime converting alcohol byproducts like acetaldehyde into substances your body can eliminate. During heavy drinking, this process backs up, leaving toxic compounds circulating in your system and contributing to nausea and malaise.
