## The Science Behind NAD+ IV Drip Treatments

NAD+ is a coenzyme present in every living cell of your body. Think of it as cellular fuel—without adequate NAD+, your cells cannot efficiently convert nutrients into energy or perform essential maintenance functions. Unfortunately, modern life accelerates NAD+ depletion through stress, poor sleep, alcohol consumption, and environmental toxins.

**NAD+ plays critical roles in:**

- **Mitochondrial Function**: Powering the cellular engines that produce ATP, your body's energy currency
- **DNA Repair Mechanisms**: Maintaining genetic integrity against daily environmental damage
- **Sirtuin Activation**: Stimulating "longevity genes" associated with healthy aging
- **Neurotransmitter Synthesis**: Supporting brain chemicals essential for mood, focus, and memory
- **Circadian Rhythm Regulation**: Helping maintain healthy sleep-wake cycles
- **Metabolic Optimization**: Supporting glucose regulation and healthy weight management

**Why IV delivery matters**: Oral NAD+ supplements face significant digestive barriers—stomach acids destroy much of the compound before absorption, and what remains faces intestinal uptake limitations. **NAD+ IV therapy San Clemente** treatments bypass these obstacles entirely, delivering therapeutic concentrations directly into your bloodstream for immediate cellular availability.
