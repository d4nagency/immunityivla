## The Science of NAD+ and Cellular Longevity

NAD+ is more than just an energy booster; it is a fundamental component of cellular metabolism and repair. It plays a vital role in several critical biological processes:

1.  **ATP Production:** NAD+ is essential for the mitochondria—the powerhouses of your cells—to convert nutrients into adenosine triphosphate (ATP), the primary energy currency of the body.
2.  **DNA Repair:** NAD+ activates PARPs (poly ADP-ribose polymerases), enzymes that are critical for repairing damaged DNA, helping to protect against cellular aging and dysfunction.
3.  **Sirtuin Activation:** NAD+ is a necessary cofactor for sirtuins, a family of proteins often referred to as "longevity genes." Sirtuins help regulate cellular health, aging, and metabolic processes.
4.  **Cognitive Function:** By supporting mitochondrial health in the brain, NAD+ helps mitigate cognitive decline and improves mental clarity and focus.
