## The Science of Hangovers — And Why IV Therapy Works

Understanding what causes a hangover helps explain why **IV therapy for hangover recovery** is so effective. Alcohol consumption triggers multiple physiological responses that leave you feeling miserable:

**Dehydration:** Alcohol is a potent diuretic, causing your body to lose fluids and electrolytes faster than you can replace them through drinking water. This fluid loss creates the headache, dizziness, and dry mouth that define the hangover experience.

**Vitamin Depletion:** Your body rapidly burns through B-vitamins, vitamin C, and magnesium while processing alcohol. This nutrient drain contributes to fatigue, brain fog, and the general sense of feeling unwell.

**Toxin Accumulation:** As your liver metabolizes alcohol, it produces acetaldehyde — a toxic compound that's significantly more harmful than alcohol itself and responsible for many hangover symptoms including nausea and inflammation.

**Gastrointestinal Distress:** Alcohol irritates your stomach lining and increases acid production, leading to nausea, stomach pain, and reduced appetite.

**Sleep Disruption:** While alcohol may help you fall asleep initially, it severely disrupts REM sleep, leaving you tired even after a full night in bed.

**Why IV Therapy is Superior:**
When you're hungover, your digestive system is compromised. Nausea makes it difficult to keep down water or oral supplements, and even when you can, absorption through your gut is slow and inefficient. **Hangover IV therapy** bypasses your digestive system entirely, delivering fluids, electrolytes, vitamins, and medications directly into your bloodstream for immediate bioavailability and faster relief.
