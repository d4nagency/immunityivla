## What is NAD+ and Why It Matters

Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide (NAD+) is a coenzyme found in every cell and essential for life. This remarkable molecule plays critical roles in:

**Cellular Energy Production**: NAD+ is key in mitochondria — your cells' powerhouses. Without adequate NAD+, cells cannot efficiently convert nutrients into ATP. When levels decline, you experience fatigue and mental fog.

**DNA Repair**: NAD+ activates sirtuins and PARPs — proteins responsible for DNA repair and cellular maintenance. Higher levels support your body's ability to repair itself.

**Anti-Aging**: Research shows maintaining optimal NAD+ levels may slow aspects of aging by supporting mitochondrial function and reducing oxidative stress.

**Cognitive Function**: The brain consumes 20% of daily energy. NAD+ supports neuronal health, memory formation, and cognitive clarity.

Unfortunately, NAD+ levels naturally decline with age — dropping by 50% by middle age. This decline contributes to decreased energy, cognitive decline, and metabolic disorders. **NAD+ IV therapy in Hacienda Heights** directly addresses this.
