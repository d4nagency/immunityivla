## Understanding NAD+ and Cellular Health

NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) is a coenzyme found in every cell of your body. It's essential for:

- **Cellular Energy Production**: NAD+ is critical for mitochondrial function—the process that converts nutrients into ATP, your cells' energy currency
- **DNA Repair**: NAD+ activates sirtuins ("longevity genes") and supports PARP enzymes that repair DNA damage
- **Healthy Aging**: Maintaining NAD+ levels helps combat the cellular decline associated with aging
- **Cognitive Function**: Brain cells require significant energy; NAD+ supports mental clarity and focus
- **Metabolic Health**: NAD+ plays a key role in regulating metabolism and maintaining healthy weight

Unfortunately, NAD+ levels decline naturally with age—dropping by approximately 50% by age 40 and continuing to decrease throughout life. Environmental stressors, poor sleep, alcohol consumption, and chronic stress further deplete NAD+ stores. **NAD+ IV Therapy in View Park-Windsor Hills** directly restores these critical levels, supporting optimal cellular function.
