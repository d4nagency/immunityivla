## The Power of NAD+: Why Cellular Health Matters

Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide (NAD+) is a coenzyme found in every single cell of your body. It plays a critical role in fundamental biological processes, most notably in the production of ATP (adenosine triphosphate), which is the primary energy currency of your cells. 

As we age, or as we subject our bodies to the stresses of modern life—such as poor sleep, high-stress environments, or even moderate alcohol consumption—our natural NAD+ levels begin to decline. This depletion is often linked to the symptoms many of us recognize as "aging": decreased energy, cognitive fog, slower recovery, and a general decline in metabolic efficiency.
