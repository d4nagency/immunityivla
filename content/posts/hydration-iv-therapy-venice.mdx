## Hydration Tips for Venice Residents

While IV therapy provides rapid rehydration, maintaining good daily habits extends the benefits:

1. **Start your day hydrated:** Drink 16-20oz of water upon waking, especially before morning beach activities
2. **Pre-hydrate before exercise:** Don't wait until you're thirsty during that bike ride to Malibu
3. **Add electrolytes:** During hot days or intense workouts, water alone isn't enough
4. **Limit dehydrating beverages:** Coffee and alcohol are part of Venice culture — balance them with extra water
5. **Eat water-rich foods:** Local farmers markets offer hydrating fruits and vegetables
6. **Monitor urine color:** Pale yellow indicates good hydration; dark yellow suggests you need fluids
