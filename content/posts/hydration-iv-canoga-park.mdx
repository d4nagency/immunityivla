## The Science Behind IV Hydration

Unlike drinking water or sports drinks, **IV hydration therapy** delivers fluids directly into your bloodstream. This method ensures immediate cellular hydration without the delays of digestion. When you're severely dehydrated from Valley heat or intense physical activity, your body can only absorb a limited amount of fluid through the gastrointestinal tract. IV therapy circumvents this limitation, delivering hydration exactly where it's needed.
