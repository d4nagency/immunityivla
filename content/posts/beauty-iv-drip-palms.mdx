### Key Beauty Nutrients Explained

**Vitamin C for Collagen**
Your body needs Vitamin C to produce collagen—the protein that gives skin structure and elasticity. Without adequate Vitamin C, collagen production slows, leading to fine lines and sagging. Our high-dose therapy supports robust collagen synthesis.

**Glutathione for Brightening**
Often called the "master antioxidant," glutathione helps regulate melanin production. Higher glutathione levels correlate with brighter, more even skin tone. It also supports detoxification, helping clear skin from within.

**B-Vitamins for Vitality**
Biotin deficiency leads to dry skin and hair. B12 supports cellular energy and renewal. B5 aids skin hydration. Together, B-complex vitamins support the "vitality glow" that signifies health.

**Hydration for Plumpness**
Dehydrated skin appears dull and emphasizes fine lines. Proper hydration from within plumps skin cells, creating that youthful, dewy appearance no cream can replicate.
