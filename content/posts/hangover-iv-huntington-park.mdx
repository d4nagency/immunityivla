## Understanding the Science of Hangovers

**Hangovers aren't just about drinking too much — they're a complex physiological response.** Alcohol acts as a diuretic, causing your body to lose fluids and electrolytes at an accelerated rate. It also triggers inflammation, irritates your stomach lining, disrupts sleep patterns, and depletes essential nutrients including B-vitamins, magnesium, and glutathione.

In Huntington Park's warm climate, these effects are often amplified. The combination of outdoor celebrations, dancing, physical activity, and Southern California heat creates pre-existing dehydration that alcohol consumption worsens. A casual night out with friends can leave you waking up with pounding headaches, nausea, and fatigue that make it impossible to enjoy your weekend.

**Common hangover symptoms our IV therapy addresses:**

- Dehydration headaches and migraines
- Nausea and stomach upset
- Fatigue and lethargy
- Brain fog and difficulty concentrating
- Muscle aches and weakness
- Dizziness and lightheadedness
- Anxiety and irritability
- Sensitivity to light and sound
