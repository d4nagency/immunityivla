## The Science Behind IV Hydration vs. Drinking Water

### Why Oral Hydration Falls Short

When you drink water, your digestive system processes it slowly. Under optimal conditions, your body absorbs only about 50-60% of the fluids you consume orally—and that's if you're perfectly healthy. Factors like exercise, heat exposure, illness, or even stress can reduce absorption significantly. What your body doesn't absorb passes through as waste.

### IV Hydration Advantages

**Immediate Absorption** — IV fluids bypass the gastrointestinal tract entirely, entering directly into your bloodstream. This means 100% bioavailability and immediate cellular hydration.

**Electrolyte Precision** — Our **Mar Vista hydration IV** formulations include precisely calibrated electrolytes that match your body's needs, something nearly impossible to achieve by drinking even the best sports drinks.

**Vitamin Co-delivery** — Unlike oral supplements that lose potency through digestion, IV-delivered vitamins reach your cells in full strength while hydrating simultaneously.

**Volume Capacity** — Consuming 1 liter of water rapidly can cause discomfort or nausea. IV delivery provides this volume comfortably and efficiently, making it ideal when you're significantly dehydrated.
