## The Science Behind NAD+ Therapy in Santa Monica

### Cellular Energy Production

NAD+ is essential for mitochondrial function—the process by which your cells generate ATP, the energy currency of life. As NAD+ levels decline, cellular energy production suffers, leading to the fatigue and decreased vitality associated with aging. NAD+ IV therapy directly restores these critical levels, revitalizing your cellular power plants.

### DNA Repair and Longevity

NAD+ activates sirtuins, a family of proteins often called "longevity genes." These proteins play crucial roles in DNA repair, cellular stress resistance, and metabolic regulation. By boosting NAD+ levels, you're essentially turning up your body's natural anti-aging mechanisms.

### Cognitive Enhancement

Your brain is one of the most energy-hungry organs in your body. NAD+ supports neuronal health, neurotransmitter production, and cognitive function. Many Santa Monica clients report improved mental clarity, focus, and memory following NAD+ therapy.
