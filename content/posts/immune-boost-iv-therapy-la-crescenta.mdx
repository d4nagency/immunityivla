## Understanding How Immune Boost IV Therapy Works

### Your Immune System: The Body's Defense Network

Your immune system is a complex network of cells, tissues, and organs working together to defend against infections and disease. Key components include:

**White Blood Cells (Leukocytes)** — Your immune system's soldiers, constantly patrolling for invaders
**Antibodies** — Specialized proteins that identify and neutralize specific pathogens
**Lymphatic System** — Network that transports immune cells and filters harmful substances
**Bone Marrow** — Where immune cells are produced
**Thymus and Spleen** — Organs that process and activate immune responses

When functioning optimally, this system identifies and destroys bacteria, viruses, and other harmful organisms before they can cause illness. However, modern life presents numerous challenges to immune function:

**Chronic Stress** — Elevated cortisol suppresses immune responses
**Poor Sleep** — Sleep deprivation dramatically reduces immune cell effectiveness
**Nutritional Deficiencies** — Standard diets often lack immune-critical nutrients
**Environmental Toxins** — Pollution, chemicals, and processed foods burden immune function
**Sedentary Lifestyles** — Physical activity supports immune circulation and function
**Aging** — Natural immune decline begins in our 30s and accelerates with age

**Vitamin C: The Immune Powerhouse**

Perhaps the most well-known immune nutrient, vitamin C supports immunity through multiple pathways:
