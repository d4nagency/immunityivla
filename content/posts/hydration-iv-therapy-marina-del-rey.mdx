## The Science Behind IV Hydration

### Why IV Hydration Outperforms Drinking Water

When you drink water, it must pass through your digestive system before reaching your cells. This process:

- Takes time (30-60 minutes for absorption)
- Loses fluids to digestive processes
- Provides limited electrolyte replacement
- Can cause discomfort when dehydrated

**IV hydration delivers fluids directly to your bloodstream**, providing:

- **100% absorption** — Every drop reaches your cells
- **Immediate availability** — Hydration begins instantly
- **Precise electrolyte balance** — Medical-grade formulation
- **Comfortable administration** — No stomach discomfort

### The Role of Electrolytes

Proper hydration requires more than just water. Electrolytes—including sodium, potassium, magnesium, and calcium—regulate:

- **Nerve function** — Communication between brain and body
- **Muscle contraction** — Including your heart muscle
- **Fluid balance** — How water moves between cells
- **pH regulation** — Maintaining proper body chemistry
