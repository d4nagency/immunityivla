## What is NAD+ IV Therapy?

NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) is an essential coenzyme present in every cell of your body. Often called the "molecule of youth," NAD+ is fundamental to:

- **Cellular Energy Production** — Powers mitochondrial function and ATP synthesis
- **DNA Repair & Cellular Maintenance** — Supports the body's natural repair mechanisms
- **Cognitive Enhancement** — Improves focus, mental clarity, and memory retention
- **Longevity Activation** — Stimulates sirtuins, known as the "longevity genes"

Unfortunately, NAD+ levels naturally decline by approximately 50% by age 40, contributing to decreased energy, cognitive decline, and visible aging.
