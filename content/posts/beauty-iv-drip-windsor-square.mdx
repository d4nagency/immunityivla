## The Science Behind Beauty IV Drip Therapy

### Why IV Delivery for Beauty Works

Your skin is your body's largest organ — and its health reflects your internal wellness. While topical products work on the surface, true skin transformation requires nourishment from within. Here's why IV delivery is the most effective approach:

**100% Bioavailability** — Oral supplements must survive stomach acid, digestive enzymes, and liver processing. Even the best formulations achieve only 10-30% absorption. IV delivery puts nutrients directly into your bloodstream, ensuring complete availability to your cells.

**Immediate Cellular Access** — Nutrients reach skin cells within minutes, versus hours or days with oral supplements. This means faster, more visible results.

**Therapeutic Doses** — IV therapy can deliver doses that would be impossible to absorb orally. For example, our 15-25g vitamin C doses would cause significant digestive upset if taken by mouth but are well-tolerated and highly effective via IV.

**Sustained Glutathione Levels** — Oral glutathione is largely destroyed by digestion. IV glutathione achieves optimal blood levels for hours, providing extended antioxidant and brightening benefits.
