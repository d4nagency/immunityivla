## What Is the Myers' Cocktail?

Developed in the 1960s by Dr. John Myers, a physician from Baltimore, the Myers' Cocktail represents the foundation of modern IV nutrient therapy. This pioneering formula demonstrated that intravenous delivery of vitamins and minerals could achieve results impossible with oral supplementation alone.

**The Classic Myers' Cocktail Formula:**

- **Vitamin C (5-10g)** — A powerful antioxidant that supports immune function, collagen production, and cellular protection against oxidative stress
- **B-Complex Vitamins** — The full spectrum of B vitamins (B1, B2, B3, B5, B6) that fuel cellular energy production, nervous system function, and metabolic processes
- **Vitamin B12 (Methylcobalamin)** — Essential for red blood cell formation, neurological function, and sustained energy without the jitters of caffeine
- **Magnesium** — A critical mineral involved in over 300 enzymatic reactions, supporting muscle function, stress response, and energy metabolism
- **Calcium** — Important for cellular signaling, muscle contraction, and bone health
- **Zinc** — Supports immune function, wound healing, and cellular repair processes

**Why IV Delivery Matters:**

Oral supplements must navigate the digestive system, where absorption rates vary dramatically based on gut health, timing, and individual biochemistry. Many people absorb only 20-50% of oral nutrients. The Myers' Cocktail delivered intravenously achieves 100% bioavailability — every molecule enters your bloodstream directly, available for immediate cellular use.

This direct delivery creates therapeutic blood concentrations impossible to achieve with pills or capsules, which is why the Myers' Cocktail produces such noticeable, reliable results.
