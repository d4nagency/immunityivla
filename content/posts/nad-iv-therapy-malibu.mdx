## The Science Behind NAD+ Therapy in Malibu

### Cellular Energy Production

NAD+ is essential for mitochondrial function—the process by which your cells generate ATP, the energy currency of life. NAD+ activates sirtuins, a family of proteins often called "longevity genes." These proteins play crucial roles in DNA repair, cellular stress resistance, and metabolic regulation. By boosting NAD+ levels, you're essentially turning up your body's natural anti-aging mechanisms.

### Cognitive Enhancement

Your brain is one of the most energy-hungry organs in your body. NAD+ supports neuronal health, neurotransmitter production, and cognitive function. Many Malibu clients report improved mental clarity, focus, and memory following NAD+ therapy.
