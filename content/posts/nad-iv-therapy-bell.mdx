## What Is NAD+ IV Therapy?

**NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide)** is a coenzyme found in every cell of your body. It plays a critical role in:

- **Energy production** — Powers cellular metabolism and ATP generation
- **DNA repair** — Supports cellular maintenance and longevity
- **Cognitive function** — Enhances mental clarity and focus
- **Anti-aging processes** — Promotes cellular regeneration

Unfortunately, NAD+ levels decline naturally with age—dropping by up to 50% by middle age. This decline correlates with decreased energy, slower recovery, and reduced cognitive performance. **NAD+ IV therapy** replenishes these levels directly through intravenous infusion, bypassing the digestive system for maximum absorption and effectiveness.
