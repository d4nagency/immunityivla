## What Is a Myers' Cocktail?

The Myers' Cocktail represents decades of clinical experience refined into a precise formulation of essential nutrients. Unlike oral supplements that must survive the harsh environment of your digestive tract, IV delivery ensures complete bioavailability.

### The Classic Formulation

**Vitamin C (5,000-25,000mg)** — This powerful antioxidant supports immune function, collagen synthesis, and combats oxidative stress. High doses achievable through IV therapy far exceed what oral consumption can provide.

**B-Complex Vitamins** — Including B1 (thiamine), B2 (riboflavin), B3 (niacin), B5 (pantothenic acid), and B6 (pyridoxine), these essential nutrients support energy metabolism, nervous system function, and stress response.

**Vitamin B12 (Hydroxocobalamin)** — Critical for neurological function, red blood cell formation, and DNA synthesis. Many University Park residents, particularly those with dietary restrictions common among students, may be deficient in this vital nutrient.

**Magnesium** — This essential mineral participates in over 300 biochemical reactions in the body. It supports muscle and nerve function, regulates blood sugar, and promotes healthy blood pressure. Magnesium also has a calming effect, helping manage the stress of academic and professional demands.

**Calcium** — Important for bone health, muscle function, and cardiovascular wellness, calcium rounds out the Myers' Cocktail formulation.

### Why IV Delivery Outperforms Oral Supplements

**100% Absorption** — Oral supplements face significant barriers: stomach acid degradation, digestive enzyme breakdown, and limited intestinal absorption capacity. IV therapy delivers nutrients directly to your bloodstream.

**Higher Doses** — The quantities of Vitamin C and B vitamins in a Myers' Cocktail would cause significant digestive upset if taken orally. IV administration avoids these limitations.

**Immediate Availability** — Oral supplements require hours for digestion and absorption. IV nutrients are immediately available to your cells, providing faster results.

**Bypassing Malabsorption** — Stress, poor diet, and certain medications common in student and professional life can impair nutrient absorption. IV therapy overcomes these barriers completely.
