## The Science Behind NAD+ and Cellular Health

### What is NAD+?

Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide (NAD+) is a coenzyme found in every living cell. It plays essential roles in:

- **Energy metabolism** — Converts food into cellular energy (ATP)
- **DNA repair** — Supports cellular maintenance and longevity pathways
- **Gene expression** — Regulates sirtuins, the "longevity genes"
- **Oxidative stress response** — Protects cells from free radical damage
- **Neurological function** — Maintains brain cell health and cognitive clarity

### Why IV Delivery Matters

Oral NAD+ supplements face significant absorption barriers. The digestive system breaks down most NAD+ before it reaches your cells. IV therapy delivers NAD+ directly to your bloodstream, achieving plasma concentrations impossible through oral administration.
