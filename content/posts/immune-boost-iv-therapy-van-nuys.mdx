## The Science Behind Immune Boost IV Therapy

Your immune system is your body's defense network, constantly working to identify and neutralize threats. But modern life — stress, poor sleep, processed foods, environmental toxins — can deplete the very nutrients your immune system needs to function optimally.

**Why Oral Supplements Fall Short:**
When you take vitamins by mouth, they must pass through your digestive system, where absorption rates vary wildly (often just 20-50%). Factors like gut health, stomach acid levels, and recent meals all impact how much actually reaches your bloodstream.

**Why IV Therapy Works Better:**
**Immune boost IV therapy** bypasses the digestive tract entirely, delivering nutrients directly into your bloodstream with 100% bioavailability. This means your cells receive the full therapeutic dose immediately — exactly when your immune system needs it most.

**Key Immune-Supporting Nutrients:**

**Vitamin C (High Dose)** — The immune system's best friend. Vitamin C supports white blood cell production, acts as a powerful antioxidant, and helps your body fight infections more effectively. High doses achievable through IV therapy simply aren't possible with oral supplementation.

**Zinc** — Essential for immune cell development and communication. Zinc deficiency is linked to increased susceptibility to infections and longer recovery times.

**Vitamin D** — Often called the "sunshine vitamin," Vitamin D plays a crucial role in immune regulation. Many indoor workers and Valley residents are deficient, especially during shorter winter days.

**B-Complex Vitamins** — Support energy production and cellular function. When you're fighting illness, your B vitamins get depleted quickly.

**Glutathione** — The body's master antioxidant, glutathione helps neutralize free radicals and supports detoxification pathways — freeing up immune resources.
