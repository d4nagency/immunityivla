### The Science of Hangovers (And Why IVs Work Faster)

Hangovers aren't simply about alcohol consumption—they're your body's response to multiple simultaneous stressors:

- **Dehydration**: Alcohol is a diuretic, causing your body to purge fluids and electrolytes faster than you replace them
- **Nutrient Depletion**: Your liver burns through B-vitamins and antioxidants processing alcohol
- **Toxin Buildup**: Acetaldehyde, alcohol's toxic metabolite, triggers inflammation, nausea, and headaches
- **Gastrointestinal Irritation**: Alcohol irritates your stomach lining and disrupts digestion
- **Sleep Disruption**: While alcohol may help you fall asleep, it severely reduces sleep quality

Our West Hollywood hangover IV therapy bypasses your compromised digestive system entirely, delivering fluids, medications, and nutrients directly into your bloodstream. The result: relief in 30-60 minutes instead of the 6-10 hours your body needs to recover naturally.
