## The Science of IV Hydration vs. Oral Rehydration

Understanding why **IV hydration therapy** outperforms drinking water or sports drinks helps explain its popularity among Huntington Beach's active population.

**Immediate Bioavailability:**
Oral fluids must pass through the digestive system before reaching your bloodstream — a process that takes hours and loses significant volume to digestive processes. IV fluids enter circulation immediately, with 100% of the administered volume available for cellular hydration.

**Electrolyte Precision:**
Sports drinks contain limited electrolyte varieties in approximate concentrations. IV solutions provide medical-grade electrolyte balances calibrated for rapid cellular uptake.

**Volume Tolerance:**
When severely dehydrated, many people struggle to drink sufficient volumes of water to restore balance. IV therapy bypasses this limitation entirely, delivering therapeutic hydration volumes directly.

**Efficiency for Active Lifestyles:**
Surfing or competing with a stomach full of water is uncomfortable and impractical. IV hydration provides the benefits without the physical burden of drinking liters of fluid.
