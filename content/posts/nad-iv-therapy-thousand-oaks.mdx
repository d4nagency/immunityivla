### Understanding NAD+ and Cellular Health

NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) is a coenzyme found in every living cell and is essential for hundreds of biological processes. Often called the "molecule of youth," NAD+ plays critical roles in:

- **Cellular Energy Production** — NAD+ is fundamental to mitochondrial function, helping convert nutrients into ATP, your cells' energy currency
- **DNA Repair** — Essential for repairing damaged DNA and maintaining cellular integrity
- **Gene Expression** — Regulates sirtuins, proteins associated with longevity and healthy aging
- **Cognitive Function** — Supports brain cell health, neurotransmitter production, and mental clarity
- **Metabolic Health** — Helps regulate metabolism, insulin sensitivity, and healthy weight management

Unfortunately, NAD+ levels decline by approximately 50% by age 40 and continue dropping with each decade. This decline is associated with decreased energy, cognitive fog, slower recovery, and visible signs of aging. NAD+ IV therapy delivers this vital coenzyme directly into your bloodstream, bypassing digestive breakdown and providing immediate cellular availability.
