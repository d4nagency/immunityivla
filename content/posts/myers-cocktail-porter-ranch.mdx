## Myers' Cocktail vs. Oral Supplements

You might wonder why you should choose IV therapy over simply taking vitamin pills. The answer lies in absorption and effectiveness. When you swallow supplements, they must pass through your digestive system, where stomach acid and digestive enzymes break down a significant portion of the nutrients before they ever reach your bloodstream.

Myers' Cocktail IV therapy bypasses the digestive system entirely, delivering 100% of the nutrients directly into your bloodstream for maximum absorption and immediate availability to your cells.
