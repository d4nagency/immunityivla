## Understanding Myers' Cocktail: The Original IV Nutrient Therapy

Developed in the 1960s by Dr. John Myers, a physician from Baltimore, Maryland, the **Myers' Cocktail** has become the foundation of modern IV nutrient therapy. ### What's In a Myers' Cocktail?

The classic Myers' Cocktail contains a precise blend of essential nutrients:

**Magnesium Chloride**
A critical mineral involved in over 300 biochemical reactions in the body. Magnesium supports muscle and nerve function, regulates blood pressure, and plays a crucial role in energy production. Many Toluca Lake residents discover they're magnesium deficient — a common issue that contributes to fatigue, muscle cramps, anxiety, and sleep disturbances.

**Calcium Gluconate**
Essential for bone health, nerve transmission, and muscle function. The calcium in Myers' Cocktail supports cardiovascular health and neuromuscular coordination — important for maintaining the active lifestyle Toluca Lake residents enjoy.

**Vitamin C (Ascorbic Acid)**
A powerful antioxidant that supports immune function, collagen synthesis, and cellular repair. At the concentrations used in Myers' Cocktail (typically 5-10 grams), Vitamin C cannot be effectively absorbed through oral supplementation, making IV delivery essential for therapeutic effects.

**B-Complex Vitamins**
Including B1 (Thiamine), B2 (Riboflavin), B3 (Niacin), B5 (Pantothenic Acid), and B6 (Pyridoxine). These water-soluble vitamins are essential for converting food into energy, supporting brain function, and maintaining healthy skin, hair, and nails.

**Methylcobalamin B12**
The active form of Vitamin B12 that supports nerve health, red blood cell formation, and DNA synthesis. B12 is particularly important for energy metabolism and cognitive function — two areas where Toluca Lake professionals often seek support.
