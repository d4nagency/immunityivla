## The Science of NAD+ and Its Role in Your Longevity

Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide (NAD+) is a crucial coenzyme found in every living cell in your body. It plays a fundamental role in energy production (ATP), DNA repair, and regulating circadian rhythms. It is essentially the fuel that allows your mitochondria—the powerhouses of your cells—to function optimally. 

As we age, or due to external stressors like environmental pollutants, poor sleep, and high-stress work environments, our natural NAD+ levels begin to decline. This decline is a primary driver of many age-related issues, including decreased energy, metabolic dysfunction, and cognitive decline.
