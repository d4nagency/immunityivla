## Understanding Hangovers: The Science Behind the Misery

Before exploring how **IV therapy for hangover relief** works, let's understand what actually causes those miserable morning-after symptoms:

**Dehydration:** Alcohol is a diuretic, meaning it causes your body to lose fluids faster than you can replace them. Even moderate drinking can lead to significant dehydration, resulting in headaches, dizziness, dry mouth, and fatigue.

**Electrolyte Imbalance:** As you lose fluids, you also lose essential electrolytes like sodium, potassium, and magnesium. This imbalance contributes to muscle aches, weakness, and that general feeling of being "off."

**Nutrient Depletion:** Your body burns through B vitamins and other nutrients while processing alcohol. This depletion affects everything from energy levels to cognitive function, contributing to brain fog and sluggishness.

**Toxin Buildup:** As your liver metabolizes alcohol, it produces acetaldehyde — a toxic compound that causes many hangover symptoms including nausea, headaches, and inflammation.

**Gastrointestinal Irritation:** Alcohol irritates your stomach lining and increases acid production, leading to nausea, stomach pain, and digestive upset.

**Why IV Therapy Works Better Than Oral Remedies:**
When you're hungover, your digestive system is often compromised. Nausea makes it difficult to keep down water, sports drinks, or supplements. Even when you can consume them, absorption through your gut is slow and inefficient. **Hangover IV therapy** bypasses your digestive system entirely, delivering fluids, electrolytes, vitamins, and medications directly into your bloodstream for immediate effect. Most clients feel relief within 15-30 minutes.
