### The Science: IV Hydration vs. Oral Rehydration

You might wonder why an IV drip is more effective than simply drinking a large bottle of water or an electrolyte sports drink. The answer lies in bioavailability. When you consume fluids orally, they must pass through the digestive system, which can be slow and inefficient, especially if you are already dehydrated or stressed. This process often leaves much of the hydration and vital minerals unabsorbed.

In contrast, our Hydration IV Hollywood drips deliver essential fluids, electrolytes, and vitamins directly into your bloodstream. This means 100% bioavailability and near-instantaneous absorption. This rapid delivery is critical for those needing immediate relief from dehydration, whether caused by physical exertion, heat, or the aftermath of a long night out. By bypassing the gut, we ensure your cells receive exactly what they need to function optimally, right when they need it most.
