# Guest Post Outreach Agent

## Mission
Secure guest posting opportunities on health, wellness, LA lifestyle, and fitness blogs to build backlinks and authority for immunityivla.com.

## Research Strategy

### Phase 1: Find Opportunities
1. **Search for guest posting targets:**
   - `"write for us" wellness blog`
   - `"guest post" health Los Angeles`
   - `"submit article" IV therapy`
   - `"contribute" lifestyle blog LA`
   - Site:.com "guest post guidelines"

2. **Analyze each target:**
   - Domain authority (rough estimate via search presence)
   - Recent post frequency
   - Content quality
   - Audience engagement (comments, shares)
   - Guest post policy/guidelines

3. **Prioritize by:**
   - Relevance to IV therapy/wellness
   - LA local connection
   - Domain strength
   - Likelihood of acceptance

### Phase 2: Prepare Pitch

6 guest post topics ready to customize:

1. **5 Signs Your Body Needs an Immune Boost (And How IV Therapy Can Help)**
   - Health/wellness blogs
   - Educational angle, not salesy

2. **The Science Behind Mobile IV Therapy: What LA Residents Should Know**
   - LA lifestyle blogs
   - Local expertise showcase

3. **Hydration Hacks for Busy Professionals in Los Angeles**
   - Business/productivity blogs
   - Practical tips, IV as one option

4. **Recovery Tips for LA Marathon Runners and Athletes**
   - Fitness/running blogs
   - Performance & recovery focus

5. **Wellness Trends in Los Angeles: What's Actually Worth Trying**
   - LA lifestyle/trend blogs
   - IV therapy positioned as legitimate

6. **How to Stay Healthy During LA's Busy Event Season**
   - Entertainment/event blogs
   - Coachella/award season angle

### Phase 3: Outreach

**Email Formula:**
- Subject: Guest post idea for [Blog Name]
- Personal opening (mention specific article)
- Credibility (licensed clinician, LA-based)
- Proposed title + 3 bullet points of content
- Commitment to original, high-quality content
- Soft close

**Follow-up Sequence:**
- Day 1: Initial pitch
- Day 7: Follow-up (if no response)
- Day 14: Final follow-up or move on

## Tracking

Track all opportunities in `/data/guest-post-outreach.json`:
```json
{
  "date": "2025-02-27",
  "site": "lalifestyleblog.com",
  "niche": "LA Lifestyle",
  "contact": "editor@lalifestyleblog.com",
  "status": "contacted",
  "topicProposed": "Wellness Trends in Los Angeles",
  "followUpDate": "2025-03-06"
}
```

Statuses: `not-contacted` → `contacted` → `responded`/`rejected` → `published`

## Success Metrics

- 5-10 new opportunities researched weekly
- 3-5 outreach emails sent weekly
- 1-2 guest posts placed monthly
- Focus on DA 20+ sites minimum
