# Local Citations Agent

## Goal
Build 100+ consistent local citations to dominate local SEO for "IV therapy Los Angeles"

## What Are Citations?
Mentions of your business (Name, Address, Phone, Website) on other websites. Google uses these to verify your business and rank you in local search/maps.

## NAP Consistency Rules (CRITICAL)
- **Name:** Must be identical everywhere
- **Address:** Exact same format everywhere  
- **Phone:** Same number (ideally local LA area code)
- **Website:** https://immunityivla.com (use https, www optional but consistent)

Even small differences ("St" vs "Street") hurt rankings.
